Allocation Workload:
- After freeing block 2, two free blocks must appear.
- External fragmentation must be > 0%.
- best_fit should choose smallest suitable free block.
- worst_fit should choose largest free block.

Cache Tests:
- First access of any address should be MISS.
- Repeated access should generate HIT.
- FIFO eviction should remove the oldest cached address.

Metrics:
- Used + Free memory must equal Total memory.
- Allocation success rate must drop after malloc failure.
